Competition Structure

Regular Season

  • Weekly matches against other Texas schools
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate school calendars
  • Multiple skill divisions (Varsity, JV, Novice)
  • Online and LAN tournament options

Playoffs & Championships

  • Regional playoffs leading to state championships
  • Single and double elimination brackets
  • Live streaming of championship matches
  • Awards and recognition ceremonies

Academic Integration

  • GPA requirements for participation
  • STEAM curriculum connections
  • College recruitment opportunities
  • Scholarship and award programs
