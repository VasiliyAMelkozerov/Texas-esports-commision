Middle School Competition Structure

Regular Season

  • Bi-weekly matches with flexible scheduling
  • Shorter match durations appropriate for age group
  • Single skill division with mentorship program
  • Online competitions with supervised environments

Championships & Events

  • Regional celebrations leading to state championships
  • Focus on participation and improvement over rankings
  • Special themed tournaments and fun events
  • Recognition ceremonies for all participants

Educational Integration

  • Grade monitoring and academic support
  • STEAM curriculum aligned with gaming activities
  • Digital citizenship and online safety education
  • Preparation pathway to high school esports
