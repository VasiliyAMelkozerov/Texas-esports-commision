Texas Esports Commission

Educational nonprofit esports organization and league created by teachers for Texas students

Our vision is to create a world where electronic sports are embraced as a positive educational and developmental change agent for all students across Texas. We welcome all students, coaches, administrators, and parents interested in participating in esports.

Our Mission

Empower Students

Providing educational institutions with the knowledge, direction, support and resources to implement, maintain, educate and compete in electronic sports.

Educational Value

Striving to give students and schools the best possible accommodations for educational value, competition, involvement, and recruitment opportunities.

Statewide Competition

Creating opportunities for Texas students to compete for their school with esports in a structured, supportive environment.
