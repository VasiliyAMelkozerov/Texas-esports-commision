About Texas Esports Commission

Meet our team and learn about our mission to transform education through esports

Our Mission

Texas Esports Commission empowers students by providing educational institutions across Texas with the knowledge, direction, support and resources to implement, maintain, educate and compete in electronic sports.

We strive to give students and schools the best possible accommodations for educational value, competition, involvement, and recruitment opportunities.

Our Vision

To create a world where electronic sports are embraced as a positive educational and developmental change agent for all students across Texas.

We welcome all students, coaches, administrators, and parents interested in participating in this ever-growing and ever-changing group of electronic game competitions.
